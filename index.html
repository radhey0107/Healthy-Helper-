Major Chronic Diseases

Diabetes

Causes: Insulin resistance, obesity, poor diet, genetics.

Symptoms: Frequent urination, thirst, blurred vision.

Prevention: Healthy diet, regular exercise, weight control, blood sugar checks.

Hypertension

Causes: High salt intake, stress, obesity, genetics.

Symptoms: Usually silent, sometimes headache or dizziness.

Prevention: Reduce salt, exercise, avoid smoking, regular BP monitoring.

Heart Disease

Causes: High cholesterol, hypertension, smoking, diabetes.

Symptoms: Chest pain, breathlessness, fatigue.

Prevention: Healthy lifestyle, no tobacco, stress management, checkups.

COPD

Causes: Smoking, pollution, occupational exposure.

Symptoms: Chronic cough, breathlessness, chest tightness.

Prevention: Quit smoking, avoid polluted air, lung health checkups.

CKD

Causes: Diabetes, hypertension, infections, genetics.

Symptoms: Fatigue, swelling in feet, frequent urination at night.

Prevention: Control diabetes/BP, avoid excess painkillers, kidney tests.

Preventive Measures

  • Eat balanced meals with more vegetables and less processed foods.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • Manage stress with yoga, meditation, or hobbies.
  • Get regular checkups: BP, sugar, cholesterol, kidney function.
  • Take vaccines and treat infections early.
